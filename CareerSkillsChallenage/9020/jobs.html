Requirments

We are looking for compassionate and dedicated individuals to join our team at the pet adoption center. The ideal candidate should have a genuine love for animals, as well as a strong desire to help them find their forever homes. If you're someone who is patient, understanding, and enthusiastic about animal welfare, we want to hear from you.

Experience in animal care, customer service, or a related field is preferred, though we are willing to train the right person. You must have excellent communication skills and be comfortable interacting with potential pet adopters. The ability to stay calm and provide clear information in high-pressure situations is a key aspect of the role.

Applicants should be flexible with scheduling, as weekend and evening shifts may be required. A positive attitude, teamwork, and a strong work ethic are essential in providing the best experience for both animals and visitors. This is a rewarding opportunity for anyone passionate about making a difference in the lives of pets and their new families.
